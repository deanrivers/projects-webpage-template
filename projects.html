Shelves


Our "Shelves" are desgined to recreate the fun and joy of the physical shopping experience. A respondent can zoom in on virtual products he/she is interested in and select items for "purchase" by placing them in a shopping cart.

Our "Hot Spots" question type can be used to visually represent choices on an existing product, product shelves or store aisles. With the use of our in-house "Hot Spot" generator application, any photo can be transformed into a responsive "Hot Spot" question.

Mobile Custom Rating


Our "Mobile Custom Rating" design has been used in surveys to add a slick feel to the survey-taking process. When the screen becomes too narrow for a typical custom rating question, our responsive design transforms the question for a mobile-friendly experince.
